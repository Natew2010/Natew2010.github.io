Bunnies

January 12, 2022

Bunnies are so cute and fluffy! They are one of the best pets in the world. They love snuggling! If you want to get one as a pet, here are some things to know: first, they do NOT eat carrots, like myth has it. They are a sugary treat for them! Do not feed them to much. Instead, rabbits LOVE greens like lettuce.

Cats

January 12, 2022

Cats are so soft, and they love playing with you! You can give them balls, Pom-Poms, or little mice toys. They eat wet and dry cat food. Make sure you dont accadentally eat some! Ew!!! They also like chicken.

Hermit Crabs

January 12, 2022

Hermit crabs aren't much fun. But they're interesting to watch. They go underground for months at a time, and burrow down there. When they do come up, they mostly just drink water. Also, sometimes, when they outgrow their shell, they pick a new one that fits, if theres one lying around.
